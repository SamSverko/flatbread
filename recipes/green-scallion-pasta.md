## Ingredients

- 4 servings rigatoni
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 Tbsp sesame seeds
- 3 bunch scallion
- chili crisp
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
- 6 oz bag baby spinach
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1/2 lemon juiced
- ricotta

## Directions

1. In salted boiling water, quickly blanch the green parts of the scallions, baby spinach, and garlic cloves (about 45 sec).
1. Remove and immediately add to an ice bath to cool it down and stop from cooking further.
1. Drain and add blanched greens to a blender with the parm, sesame seeds, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper, and puree.
1. Now cook your pasta noodles according to the box instructions until preferred doneness, then strain and set aside.
1. Pour your green sauce into a pan, add the pasta noodles, a few Tbsp of pasta water, butter, and mix.
1. Finally, plate by spooning onto a bowl or plate and topping with a dollop of ricotta and a drizzle of chili crisp.
