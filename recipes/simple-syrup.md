## Ingredients

-   ½ cup sugar
-   ½ cup water

## Directions

1. In a small saucepan, bring sugar and water to a boil, stirring until sugar is completely dissolved.
1. Set aside; let cool completely.

## Notes

-   Make-ahead: Can be covered and refrigerated for up to 1 month.
