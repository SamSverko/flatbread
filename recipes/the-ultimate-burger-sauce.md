## Ingredients

-   ½ cup mayonnaise
-   3 tbsp ketchup
-   3 tbsp finely chopped dill pickle
-   1 tsp mustard
-   ¼ tsp Worcestershire sauce
-   ¼ tsp salt

## Directions

1. In small bowl, stir together mayonnaise, ketchup, pickle, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and salt until combined.
