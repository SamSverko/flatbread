## Ingredients

### Marinade

- 2 lbs chicken thighs
- 5 cloves garlic
- 1/2 shallot
- 1/4 cup yogurt
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- Zest and juice of a lemon

### Seasonings

- 1 packet Sazon (blend of blend of coriander, cumin, achiote, garlic powder, oregano, salt & pepper)
- 1 tsp chipotle chili powder
- 2 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- Salt to taste

### Toppings

- Tomato
- Red onion
- Pita

## Directions

1. Bake for 20 minutes at 400F, flip and finish at 425F ~ took about 10-15 more minutes
